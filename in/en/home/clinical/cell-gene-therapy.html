          Featured cell and gene therapy products

          Gibco CTS Rotea Counterflow Centrifugation System

          The multipurpose Gibco CTS Rotea Counterflow Centrifugation System offers exceptional flexibility in a cell therapy development and manufacturing setting. This versatile system applies the proven counterflow centrifugation methods to a broad range of cell processing applications, such as CAR-T therapy, stem cell therapy, and PBMC isolation.

          Learn more

          Gibco CTS OpTmizer Pro

          Gibco CTS OpTmizer Pro SFM, no phenol red, is a novel serum-free medium (SFM) developed to support culture and expansion of human T lymphocytes (e.g., CD4+, CD8+, polyclonal, or antigen specific) used in allogeneic cell therapies. This medium improves central memory phenotype and cell growth by shifting the cells metabolism. The result is a larger population of central memory cells in a shorter period of time.

          Learn more

          Thermo Scientific Heracell Vios CR CO2 Incubator CTS Series

          First of its kind - this third party certified, cleanroom compatible CO2 incubator is proven to be suitable for use in ISO Class 5 and GMP Grade A/B environments. This series is designed to reduce particle emissions, improve cleanability, and provide complete documentation to support demanding applications like gene & cell therapy.

          Learn more
