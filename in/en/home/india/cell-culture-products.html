          Start-to-end solutions for your next cell culture discovery

          Cell culture is the building block in life sciences research on all platforms; whether to produce recombinant proteins or virus particles or to make cell models. Offering you smooth, hassle-free workflow solutions at every stage of your cell culture with a complete product portfolio to deliver the best results.

          Choose from a range of products available at Thermo Fisher to accelerate your research for every step of cell culture workflow.

          Media Preparation

          Powdered media is dissolved in Type I water, pH is adjusted, followed by filter sterilization. Stirring and filtration can be performed in biological safety cabinets. Alternatively, ready-made liquid media can be used.

          Cryopreservation & Storage

          After washing and centrifuging, they are resuspended in a storage medium in a cryovial. After being cooled to -80°C, they are stored in liquid N2 and can be preserved for years.
