With our Invitrogen and Gibco portfolios of protein kinases, ADME/Tox products, primary & stem cell products, target-based assays, and custom biology services we help you drive efficiency in assay development, high-throughput screening, and metabolic and safety assessment.

          Most popular Drug Discovery & Development products & resources

          Spheroid-Qualified Hepatocytes

          Ready to plate in a single 60-min session, for cultures lasting >4x traditional 2D mononlayer culture.

          HepExtend Supplement

          Extends functional life of primary hepatocytes and enhances metabolic activity, enabling more predictive results and experimental flexibility

          SelectScreen Services

          Profiling and screening services with rapid turnaround times!

          Rapid Equilibrium Dialysis

          Offers an easy, fast and reliable system for performing plasma protein-binding assays.

          Featured Drug Discovery & Development categories

          Drug discovery & development services

          Expand your resources with our outsourced services for screening and profiling, custom assay development, cell engineering, and ADME/Tox testing.

          Target-based proteins & assays

          Accelerate your drug discovery research with a complete portfolio of reagents, assays, and services to investigate kinases, pathways,GPCRs, ion channels, nuclear receptors and epigenetics biology.

          Stem cell research

          From cell culture to differentiation,engineering, and analysis, we have the products to support you at every step of your stem cell research including your cell therapy applications.

          Biochemical kinase assays

          Thermo Fisher Scientific offers you the broadest set of biochemical fluorescent assays to address high-throughput screening, hit confirmation and lead optimization programs and are available as kits, custom or bulk reagents.

          Organoids, spheroids & 3D cell culture

          Gain confidence to culture and analyze organoids and spheroids through validated protocols and educational resources brought together to enable better drug discovery and development

          Target-based ADME/Tox assays

          Examine the safety risks for your compounds with our Predictor hERG assay, P450s, GPCRs, nuclear receptors, transporters, and PCR-based discovery assays.

          Drug discovery data management 

          A flexible platform solution for biologics discovery and development, with end-to-end sample management and data collection.

          Primary cell culture systems

          Human primary cell cultures closely mimic the in vivo state and generate more physiologically relevant data from tissues of interest including skin, muscle, neural, and more.

          Biomarker & discovery validation

          Comprehensive solutions for gene expression screening, profiling, and verification, with integrated data compatibility across technology platforms. Transition between targeted RNA sequencing, gene expression microarrays, and real-time PCR, depending on the stage of your research.

          GPCR extraction and stabilization

          Thermo Scientific GPCR Extraction and Stabilization Reagent enables efficient extraction and stabilization of G-coupled protein receptors (GPCRs) and other membrane-associated proteins from cultured mammalian cells or tissue while preserving longer-term receptor integrity.

          Screening compounds & libraries for hit identification

          Our screening compounds and libraries consist of a high-throughput screening library of over 53,000 hit-like, individually-designed molecules, a hit-to-lead building block collection of pharmacophorically rich intermediates specifically designed for medicinal chemistry, and fragment libraries of over 30,000 chemical fragments that include bromo-, fluoro-, and pre-fragment collections.

          High-throughput screening (HTS) for drug discovery

          This highly diverse set of over 53,000 hit- and lead-like molecules, widely acknowledged as a critical tool in screening campaigns, includes our HitFinder, HitCreator, and HitDiscover collections of pre-plated compounds, as well as highly focused libraries designed to expedite the development of drugs exhibiting specific biological activities.

          Lab ideas for medicine makers – current topics for pharma – biopharma analytical labs!

          Lab ideas for people – like yourself– in analytical labs making medicines, directed by topics, ideas and thoughts from your fellow medicine makers. Each month, a new theme addresses challenges and solutions around technology and workflows.
