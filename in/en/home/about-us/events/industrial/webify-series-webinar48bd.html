          Webisode 5: Gain Lab Compliance Confidence: FDA standards of laboratory operations

          Edward Griffin | Regulatory Products Manager, Thermo Fisher Scientific
          Synopsis

          Learn the recommended equipment compliance life cycle, importance of qualification, step by step temperature mapping and cycle testing of your laboratory instruments. FDA regulations for complete laboratory compliance, calibration certification requirements and steps you need to take to ensure regulatory compliance will be discussed in this session.

          Biography

          Prior to joining Thermo Fisher Scientific Edward worked as an Analytical Scientist at a number of Pharma companies working on both small molecule and biological drugs, using a number of different instrumentations including GC, LC and MS. He joined Thermo Fisher Scientific in January 2021 as Regulatory Product Manager, Edward works closely within our Regulatory Products Support Group to create and update compliance and qualification procedures and assist our field-based engineers.

          Webisode 6: Learn the SANTE Guidelines of food safety and quality using GC-MS/MS

          Subodh Budakoti | Application Development Scientist, Thermo Fisher Scientific
          Synopsis

          SANTE Guidelines used to determine the food safety standards, typical workflows using a GC-MS/MS, 3 case studies on determining toxins in food and the best possible ways to analyze your results.

          Biography

          With close to a decade of experience managing GC, GC-MS/MS, Subodh has been working on developing and validating methods for challenging contaminates in the food matrices with various industry leaders.

          Webisode 7: Gain Lab Compliance Confidence: Meet global standards & mitigate risk

          Martin Wheeler| Regulatory Products Manager, Thermo Fisher Scientific
          Synopsis

          Learn the OEM recommended process/performance validation and qualification lifecycle, various stages of qualification. FDA, GMP & ISO regulations to ensure complete compliance, calibration certification requirements and ensuring audit quality data will be discussed in this session.

          Biography

          Since 1997, Martin has worked within the Thermo Fisher Scientific organization focusing on single quadrupole instrumentation using gas & liquid chromatography together with mass spectrometers, CDS, and layered applications. In his current role as Regulatory Product Manager, Martin works closely within our Regulatory Products Support Group to create and update compliance and qualification procedures and assist our field-based engineers.

          Webisode 8: Learn the SANTE Guidelines of food safety and quality using LC-MS/MS

          Ramiz M.R. Azad | Application Development Scientist, Thermo Fisher Scientific
          Synopsis

          SANTE Guidelines used to determine the food safety standards, typical workflows using a LC-MS/MS, 3 case studies on determining toxins in food and the best possible ways to analyze your results.

          Biography

          Awarded by Food Safety Standards Authority of India for conducting various webinars, published more than five papers in International Journals, wrote numerous Application Notes and blogs on Food Safety. Ramiz has spent over 12 years in the field of Liquid Chromatography & Mass Spectrometry with various industry leaders from Food Safety, Pharma & Clinical.

          Webisode 9: HPLC Troubleshooting & Maintenance Techniques

          Justin Philiphos| Applications Manager HPLC/UHPLC, Thermo Fisher Scientific
          Synopsis

          Learn the troubleshooting techniques of all HPLC modules Auto-sampler, pump, column compartment and detectors. Understand the best ways to analyze your data, important industrial applications of the instruments.

          Biography

          Justin has about 8 years experience in the Pharmaceutical Analytical Development and Characterization Work, and then has been supporting customers with Application Troubleshooting for Liquid Chromatography and Charged Aerosol detector with Thermo Fisher Scientific for the last 5 years. His Core expertise is into Method Translation and Method transfers, especially for Liquid Chromatographic systems (HPLC to HPLC method transfers, or HPLC to UPLC conversion). Also, supports choice techniques like Fluorescence Detection, ECD, RI, Fraction Collections, and Online 2 Dimensional LC.
